1. **Speed of Light in a Vacuum:**
   - The speed of light in a vacuum is approximately 299,792 kilometers per second (or about 3×10^8 meters per second). This constant is crucial in physics as it appears in many equations and theories, such as Einstein's theory of relativity.

2. **Novel "One Hundred Years of Solitude":**
   - The novel was written by Jhumpa Lahiri, originally published in 1987. It falls under the contemporary literary movement due to its themes on cultural identity and human existence.

3. **Photosynthesis Overview:**
   - Photosynthesis is a process where plants convert light energy into chemical energy using carbon dioxide and water to produce glucose (energy-rich sugar) and oxygen. The equation is 6CO₂ + 6H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂.

4. **Countries Bordering Germany:**
   - Germany shares borders with France, Belgium, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, and the Czech Republic. Their capitals are Paris, Brussels, Luxembourg City, Amsterdam, and Prague respectively.
