**Problem 1: Dimensions of Sarah's Garden**

*Given:*
- Let \( w \) be the width (in meters).
- Length (\( l \)) is 3 meters more than twice the width: 
  \[
  l = 2w + 3
  \]
- Perimeter (\( P \)) is 36 meters.

*Perimeter Formula for a Rectangle:*
\[
P = 2l + 2w
\]

*Substituting \( l \) and \( P \):*
\[
36 = 2(2w + 3) + 2w
\]
\[
36 = 4w + 6 + 2w
\]
\[
36 - 6 = 6w
\]
\[
30 = 6w
\]
\[
w = \frac{30}{6} = 5 \text{ meters}
\]

*Calculating Length (\( l \)):*
\[
l = 2(5) + 3 = 10 + 3 = 13 \text{ meters}
\]

**Final Dimensions:**
- Width: **5 meters**
- Length: **13 meters**

**Problem 2: Final Price of the Jacket**

*Given:*
- Original Price (\( P_0 \)) = \$120
- First Discount = 25%
- Second Discount = 10% off the sale price

*Calculating the First Discount (Sale Price):*
\[
\text{Discount Amount} = 0.25 \times 120 = \$30
\]
\[
\text{Sale Price after First Discount} = 120 - 30 = \$90
\]

*Calculating the Second Discount:*
\[
\text{Second Discount Amount} = 0.10 \times 90 = \$9
\]
\[
\text{Final Price after Second Discount} = 90 - 9 = \$81
\]

**Final Price of the Jacket:** **\$81**

**Problem 3: Evaluating \( f(4) \)**

*Given Function:*
\[
f(x) = 2x^2 - 5x + 3
\]
*Task: Find \( f(4) \).*

*Substituting \( x = 4 \):*
\[
f(4) = 2(4)^2 - 5(4) + 3
\]

*Calculating Each Term:*
\[
2(4)^2 = 2 \times 16 = 32
\]
\[
-5(4) = -20
\]
\[
+3 = +3
\]

*Adding the Results Together:*
\[
f(4) = 32 - 20 + 3 = 15
\]

**Final Value:** **\( f(4) = 15 \)**
